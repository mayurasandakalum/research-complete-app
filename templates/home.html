Mathematics Quiz App for Grade 4 & 5

Our Sinhala medium mathematics quiz application is specially designed for primary school students in grades 4 and 5. The app covers all essential mathematical concepts aligned with the Sri Lankan curriculum.

Key Features:

  • Interactive quizzes in Sinhala language
  • Step-by-step problem solving
  • Adaptive difficulty based on performance
  • Immediate feedback and explanations
  • Progress tracking for parents and teachers
  • Fun rewards and achievements system
  • Supports all learning styles

The app covers topics including basic arithmetic, fractions, decimals, measurements, geometry, and data representation.
